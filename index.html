Research agenda

Technology, attention, and responsible analytics in markets

My work asks how new technologies change what consumers search for, which alternatives they compare, and how markets become organized around emerging categories. I use digital trace data and quantitative models to study early market transformation before sales fully respond.

Job market paper

Capturing the Impact of Tesla

This project studies how the introduction of the Tesla Model 3 reshaped online search, advertising, and sales dynamics in the U.S. automobile market. Manuscript available upon request.
